Background

I am a computer programmer who teaches at Lynn Vocational Technical Institute. I have been a teacher for 12 years, before which I was a programmer at Mathworks in Natick from 1999-2009. I also had a few interesting jobs in between being a programmer as I transitioned to becoming a teacher. I also had a summer job at Solimine Metals on the Lynnway for a summer, bailing large chunks of metal in compressors.

I am really interested in networking to reach out in interested companies that would like to hire my students, and even finding professionals interested in letting us know what they are looking for in candidates that would encourage them to hire our graduates. I am also interested in any company that would be willing for us to do live work on their website either as an initial start (company hasn't had resources to dedicate to making a web presence) or doing enhancements they don't have the time or money for. We have worked on two public websites that have given our students valuable experience and I would like to expand that list.
